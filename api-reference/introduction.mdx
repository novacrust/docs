Novacrust provides a **RESTFUL API** based on simple **HTTP POST/GET requests**. Our Card Issuing APIs let you do the following:

- **Create Cards**
- **Manage Customers**

## Integration

You can integrate the **Novacrust API** into your software applications and custom workflows to power your card issuing.
